About The Website

Encourage reading is what we seek. We use illustrations and motion to simulate the book story in simple interactive experience.
The Missing Part of You website is based on a completely new idea compared to ordinary methods of displaying and selling books.

In this experimental phase, the novel was chosen as" The Forty Rules of Love by Elif Shafak".7 rules were chosen from the 40 rules of Shams Tabrizi. Those rules were pictured differently from the original story to provide a basic experience within an experience without spoiling the original novel.
